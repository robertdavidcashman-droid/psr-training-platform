# PSR Training Platform - Improvement Ideas

This document outlines ideas to enhance the platform and better achieve its goals of helping aspiring police station representatives complete their accreditation training.

## üéØ Core Training & Educational Improvements

### Content & Learning
1. **Interactive Case Studies**
   - Add video-based case studies with step-by-step walkthroughs
   - Include real anonymized case examples from successful portfolios
   - Interactive timeline showing what actions to take at each stage

2. **PACE Code Navigator**
   - Searchable, indexed PACE Code sections
   - Contextual links from questions/modules to relevant PACE sections
   - Highlight changes and updates to codes

3. **Flashcard System**
   - Spaced repetition algorithm for key concepts
   - Mobile-friendly flashcards for on-the-go learning
   - User-generated flashcard sets

4. **Video Tutorials**
   - Recorded sessions on complex topics
   - Interview technique demonstrations
   - Role-play scenario videos

5. **Latest Case Law Updates**
   - Automated feed of recent relevant case law
   - Case summaries with implications for practice
   - Searchable case law database

6. **Study Plans & Schedules**
   - Personalized study plans based on exam date
   - Daily/weekly learning goals
   - Progress reminders and notifications

### Assessment & Practice
7. **Adaptive Testing**
   - AI-powered adaptive difficulty based on performance
   - Focus on weak areas automatically
   - Personalized question recommendations

8. **Mock Exams**
   - Timed full-length practice exams
   - Exam conditions simulation
   - Detailed performance reports after completion

9. **Question Explanations Enhancement**
   - Detailed explanations with legal citations
   - Links to relevant statutes and case law
   - Visual diagrams for complex procedures

10. **Performance Analytics Enhancement**
    - Visual progress charts and graphs
    - Comparison with cohort averages (anonymous)
    - Identify knowledge gaps with recommendations

11. **Practice by Topic**
    - Dedicated practice sessions for specific areas
    - Topic-based quizzes with immediate feedback
    - Mastery tracking per topic
